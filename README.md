## Project Overview
Ecommerce_Advanced_Analysis is a detailed Python-based EDA project focusing on advanced sales analytics, customer segmentation, profitability trends, and product performance evaluation.

The project includes:
- Data cleaning & preparation  
- Time-series trends  
- Category & sub-category analysis  
- Discount vs profit relationships  
- Regional sales comparison  
- Customer segment insights  
- Delivery & logistics analysis

## Key Insights

- Sales peak consistently in **November & December** each year.  
- **Technology** and **Furniture** drive the highest revenue.  
- **Consumers** contribute over half of total sales.  
- Heavy discounts reduce profit significantly; optimal discount range is **10â€“20%**.  
- **West** region leads overall sales; **South** underperforms.  
- Some high-sales products still have **negative profits**, indicating cost or pricing issues.
